    What is your long term goal or career pathway?

    My long-term goal is to one day develop video games for my favorite sim racing companies like Polyphony, who have made the highly celebrated Gran Turismo.

    Description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

    My strengths generally come from my ability to push through a lot of work without stopping, especially after having a good meal in the morning. However, I can get lost in work and struggle when I reach a point where I'm stuck.

    What skills (non-technical core/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

    I would like to improve my ability to ask for help sooner instead of trying to figure things out on my own for too long.

    How will you manage your workload in this programme? Explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators, and industry/community representatives?

    I plan to manage my workload by maintaining open communication with my cohort and facilitators through channels like Discord and Slack. If things get overwhelming, I will take breaks and engage in activities unrelated to work.

    What will you do when things are building up and starting to get to you?

    I will take a deep breath, step away from my device, and engage in activities that help me relax and reset.

    How and where will you seek help in a timely way, and what do you expect from your Facilitation team?

    I will seek help through channels like Discord and Slack, and I expect my facilitation team to be available and supportive. My experience so far with Sprint 2 has been positive in terms of receiving assistance from everyone.
